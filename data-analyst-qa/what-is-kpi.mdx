## 🛑 El Error del Junior

"KPI es una métrica importante"—vago y circular. No explica la alineación con objetivos de negocio ni el criterio para elegir KPIs.

## 🧠 El Enfoque Curse Dev (Contra-pregunta)

* **Tú:** *"This is a foundational question that often reveals confusion in organizations. Would it help if I clarified the distinction and shared how I decide what qualifies as a true KPI?"*

## 💻 La Respuesta Técnica

**KPI (Key Performance Indicator)** vs **Métrica general**:

| KPI | Métrica General |
|-----|-----------------|
| Alineado a objetivo estratégico | Mide algo, pero sin vínculo a meta |
| Accionable: impulsa decisiones | Informativa, pero no necesariamente accionable |
| Limitados (5-10 por área) | Pueden ser decenas o cientos |
| Seguimiento frecuente (diario/semanal) | Consulta esporádica |

**Ejemplos:**
- **KPI**: Tasa de conversión (vinculada a ingresos)
- **Métrica**: Visitas totales al sitio (informativa, pero no accionable por sí sola)

## 🇺🇸 El Script en Inglés (Para la entrevista)

> *"A KPI, or Key Performance Indicator, is a specific type of metric that's directly tied to a strategic business objective. The key word is 'key'—not every metric qualifies. A KPI must meet three criteria: First, it's aligned to a critical goal—like revenue growth, customer retention, or operational efficiency. Second, it's actionable—meaning when the KPI moves, stakeholders know what decision to make or what action to take. Third, it's monitored regularly—daily or weekly—because it reflects the health of the business. A general metric, on the other hand, is any measurable quantity. It might be interesting or useful for context, but it doesn't necessarily drive decisions. For example, 'monthly recurring revenue' is a KPI for a SaaS business because it's tied to growth goals and triggers action when it changes. 'Total page views' is just a metric—it might correlate with success, but by itself, it doesn't tell you whether to pivot or double down. In my work, I always push teams to identify the vital few KPIs rather than drowning in vanity metrics."*

## 🔑 Vocabulario Clave

* **Strategic business objective:** Objetivo estratégico de negocio
* **Actionable:** Accionable
* **Stakeholders:** Partes interesadas
* **Monthly recurring revenue:** Ingresos recurrentes mensuales
* **Vanity metrics:** Métricas de vanidad
