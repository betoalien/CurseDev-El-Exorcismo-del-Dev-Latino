## 🛑 El Error del Junior

"Hubo un problema"—asume conclusión sin investigar. No menciona validación de datos, estacionalidad o cambios de definición.

## 🧠 El Enfoque Curse Dev (Contra-pregunta)

* **Tú:** *"A metric drop is a signal, not a conclusion. Would it help if I walked through my diagnostic framework for investigating these situations?"*

## 💻 La Respuesta Técnica

Antes de concluir, valida sistemáticamente:

**1. Calidad de datos**
- ¿Hay datos completos del periodo?
- ¿Problemas de tracking o ETL?
- ¿Cambios en schema o fuentes?

**2. Definición de la métrica**
- ¿Cambiaron criterios de cálculo?
- ¿Se modificó el código de tracking?

**3. Contexto temporal**
- ¿Estacionalidad esperada? (fin de semana, festivos, vacaciones)
- ¿Comparación vs periodo correcto? (YoY vs MoM)

**4. Segmentación**
- ¿La caída es general o de un segmento específico?
- ¿Cambio en mix de usuarios/canales?

**5. Contexto de negocio**
- ¿Cambios operativos? (lanzamientos, bugs, campañas)
- ¿Factores externos? (competencia, economía, noticias)

## 🇺🇸 El Script en Inglés (Para la entrevista)

> *"When I see a significant metric drop, my first rule is: don't panic, diagnose. A number going down is a signal to investigate, not a conclusion about business performance. I follow a systematic checklist. First, I validate data integrity: is the data complete, or did we have an ETL failure? Is the tracking code still firing correctly? I've seen 'drops' that were just delayed pipelines. Second, I check for definition changes: did someone modify how the metric is calculated? Third, I consider seasonality: is this a normal fluctuation for this day of week, time of month, or holiday period? I always compare year-over-year to control for this. Fourth, I segment: is the drop across the board, or isolated to one region, device, or channel? Often the overall number hides that one segment crashed while others were fine. Fifth, I talk to the business: did Marketing launch a campaign that drove low-quality traffic? Did Engineering deploy a checkout change? Were there external events like a competitor promotion or negative press? Only after ruling out data issues and understanding context do I conclude there's a real performance problem—and even then, I can usually point to the specific driver. This approach has saved me from countless false alarms and helped me focus on issues that actually mattered."*

## 🔑 Vocabulario Clave

* **Data integrity:** Integridad de datos
* **ETL failure:** Fallo de ETL
* **Tracking code:** Código de tracking
* **Seasonality:** Estacionalidad
* **Year-over-year:** Año contra año
