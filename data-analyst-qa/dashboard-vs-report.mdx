## 🛑 El Error del Junior

"El dashboard es visual y el reporte es texto"—simplista. No explica las diferencias de propósito, frecuencia ni interactividad.

## 🧠 El Enfoque Curse Dev (Contra-pregunta)

* **Tú:** *"They serve different purposes in the data ecosystem. Would it help if I broke down when I'd recommend building one versus the other?"*

## 💻 La Respuesta Técnica

| Aspecto | Dashboard | Reporte Tradicional |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Propósito** | Monitoreo continuo | Análisis puntual / revisión |
| **Frecuencia** | Actualización constante (diaria/hora) | Periódico (semanal/mensual) |
| **Interactividad** | Filtros, drill-down, tiempo real | Estático, impreso o PDF |
| **Contenido** | KPIs, métricas resumidas, visuales | Detalle extenso, tablas, narrativa |
| **Audiencia** | Operadores, managers, ejecutivos | Analistas, auditores, compliance |

**Cuándo usar cada uno:**
- **Dashboard**: Necesitas monitoreo continuo, alertas tempranas, toma de decisiones rápida
- **Reporte**: Necesitas documentación formal, análisis profundo, auditoría o cumplimiento

## 🇺🇸 El Script en Inglés (Para la entrevista)

> *"Dashboards and traditional reports both communicate data, but they serve fundamentally different purposes. A dashboard is like a car's instrument panel—it's always on, showing real-time status of critical metrics, designed for quick monitoring and immediate action. It's interactive, visual, and updates frequently. Think of it as a 'living' artifact that stakeholders check daily or even hourly. A traditional report, on the other hand, is like a printed document—it's static, comprehensive, and designed for deeper review or formal record-keeping. Reports often include detailed tables, extensive commentary, and supporting appendices. They're distributed on a schedule—monthly business reviews, quarterly board packs, annual compliance filings. In my work, I build dashboards for operational teams who need to react quickly to changes, and reports for stakeholders who need to digest information thoroughly or maintain audit trails. The key is matching the format to the use case: speed and interactivity for dashboards, depth and permanence for reports."*

## 🔑 Vocabulario Clave

* **Instrument panel:** Panel de instrumentos
* **Real-time status:** Estado en tiempo real
* **Living artifact:** Artefacto "vivo" / dinámico
* **Audit trails:** Huellas de auditoría
* **Use case:** Caso de uso
