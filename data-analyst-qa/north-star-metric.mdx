## 🛑 El Error del Junior

"Es la métrica más importante"—demasiado genérico. No explica el concepto de valor entregado al cliente ni la función de alineación de equipos.

## 🧠 El Enfoque Curse Dev (Contra-pregunta)

* **Tú:** *"The North Star Metric is a strategic concept that goes beyond just picking an important KPI. Would you like me to explain how it drives alignment across teams?"*

## 💻 La Respuesta Técnica

La **North Star Metric** (Métrica Estrella Polar) es **la** métrica principal que mejor representa el **valor que el producto entrega a los clientes**:

**Características:**
1. **Captura valor**: Refleja el "aha moment" del usuario
2. **Alinea equipos**: Todos reman en la misma dirección
3. **Predictiva**: Si esta métrica crece, el negocio crece
4. **Accionable**: Los equipos pueden influir en ella

**Ejemplos reales:**
- **Spotify**: Tiempo escuchando (no suscripciones)
- **Airbnb**: Noches reservadas (no ingresos)
- **Facebook**: Usuarios activos diarios (no registros)

## 🇺🇸 El Script en Inglés (Para la entrevista)

> *"The North Star Metric is the single most important metric for a product or business—not because it's the biggest number, but because it best captures the core value that customers derive from the product. Think of it as your true north: every team, from engineering to marketing, aligns their work around moving this metric in the right direction. What makes a good North Star? First, it reflects customer value, not just business outcomes. For Spotify, it's time spent listening—that signals users are getting value from music discovery. For Airbnb, it's nights booked, which means both hosts and guests are having successful experiences. Second, it's leading: if the North Star is growing, revenue and retention will follow. Third, it's actionable—teams can identify specific initiatives that move it. I've seen organizations transform when they shift from vanity metrics like 'total signups' to a true North Star like 'weekly active users creating value.' It forces everyone to focus on what actually matters for long-term success."*

## 🔑 Vocabulario Clave

* **True north:** Verdadero norte / guía principal
* **Customer value:** Valor para el cliente
* **Leading metric:** Métrica predictiva
* **Weekly active users:** Usuarios activos semanales
* **Long-term success:** Éxito a largo plazo
