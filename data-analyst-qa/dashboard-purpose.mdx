## 🛑 El Error del Junior

"Mostrar datos"—demasiado vago. No menciona la toma de decisiones accionable ni el monitoreo de KPIs.

## 🧠 El Enfoque Curse Dev (Contra-pregunta)

* **Tú:** *"A dashboard can serve different purposes depending on the audience. Would it help if I explained the key principles I follow when designing one?"*

## 💻 La Respuesta Técnica

El **objetivo principal de un dashboard** es mostrar información **relevante y accionable** para:

1. **Monitorear desempeño**: Seguimiento continuo de KPIs y métricas clave
2. **Detectar problemas**: Alertar sobre anomalías o desviaciones de targets
3. **Apoyar decisiones**: Proveer contexto para que stakeholders tomen acciones informadas

**Principios de diseño:**
- **Menos es más**: Mostrar solo lo esencial
- **Jerarquía visual**: Lo más importante arriba y a la izquierda
- **Contexto**: Comparativos vs targets, periodos anteriores, benchmarks
- **Actualización frecuente**: Datos frescos para decisiones oportunas

## 🇺🇸 El Script en Inglés (Para la entrevista)

> *"The primary objective of a dashboard is to enable faster, better-informed decisions by surfacing the right information at the right time. It's not about displaying all available data—it's about curating the vital few metrics that drive action. A well-designed dashboard answers three questions within seconds: Are we on track? Where are the problems? What needs attention? I follow a few principles: First, know your audience—an executive dashboard shows high-level KPIs with trend indicators, while an operational dashboard shows detailed metrics for daily management. Second, use visual hierarchy strategically—most important metrics top-left, supporting details below. Third, always provide context—a number without comparison is meaningless, so I include targets, prior period comparisons, or benchmarks. Fourth, design for scanability—a stakeholder should grasp the key insights in under 30 seconds. I've built dashboards that reduced meeting time by 50% because everyone walked in aligned on the current state."*

## 🔑 Vocabulario Clave

* **Actionable:** Accionable
* **KPIs:** Indicadores clave de desempeño
* **Visual hierarchy:** Jerarquía visual
* **Scanability:** Escaneabilidad / legibilidad rápida
* **Aligned:** Alineado
