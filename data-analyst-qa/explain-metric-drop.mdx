## 🛑 El Error del Junior

"Creo que fue por X"—especula sin datos. O peor: "no sé, déjame revisarlo" sin estructura.

## 🧠 El Enfoque Curse Dev (Contra-pregunta)

* **Tú:** *"When a stakeholder asks about a metric drop, they want confidence, not guesses. Would it help if I shared my framework for investigating and communicating findings?"*

## 💻 La Respuesta Técnica

**Marco de respuesta estructurada:**

**1. No asumir, investigar:**
- ❌ "Fue por la campaña de marketing"
- ✅ "Déjame analizar los datos y te confirmo en 1 hora"

**2. Validar datos primero:**
- ¿Es real el drop o problema de tracking/ETL?
- Comparar con fuente original si es posible

**3. Segmentar para aislar:**
- ¿Drop general o de un segmento específico?
- Breakdown por: región, canal, dispositivo, cohorte

**4. Buscar correlaciones:**
- ¿Cambios en el producto? (deployments, bugs)
- ¿Factores externos? (competencia, estacionalidad, noticias)
- ¿Cambios operativos? (campañas, pricing, políticas)

**5. Comunicar con contexto:**
- Magnitud: "15% vs semana pasada"
- Duración: "empezó el martes, continúa"
- Acción: "estoy investigando X, te actualizo a las 3pm"

## 🇺🇸 El Script en Inglés (Para la entrevista)

> *"When a stakeholder asks about a metric drop, my first priority is to respond with confidence, not speculation. I follow a structured approach. First, I validate the data itself—is this a real business change or a tracking/ETL issue? I've caught 'drops' that were just delayed pipelines. Second, I segment aggressively: is this drop across the board, or isolated to one region, device, or channel? Often the overall number hides that one segment crashed while others were fine. Third, I look for correlations: did we deploy a checkout change? Did a competitor launch a promotion? Is this normal seasonality? I check our release calendar, marketing campaigns, and historical patterns. Fourth, I communicate proactively: I'll tell the stakeholder, 'I see the 20% drop in conversion starting Tuesday. It's isolated to mobile traffic in Latin America. I'm investigating three potential causes and will update you by 3pm.' This shows I'm on top of it without committing to a premature conclusion. The worst response is guessing and being wrong—it destroys credibility. I'd rather say 'I don't know yet, but I'm finding out' and deliver a data-backed answer."*

## 🔑 Vocabulario Clave

* **Speculation:** Especulación
* **Tracking/ETL issue:** Problema de tracking/ETL
* **Segment aggressively:** Segmentar agresivamente
* **Correlations:** Correlaciones
* **Proactively:** Proactivamente
