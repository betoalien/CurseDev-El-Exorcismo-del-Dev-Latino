## 🛑 El Error del Junior

"El engineer programa más y el analyst usa Excel"—reduce ambos roles a herramientas, sin explicar el propósito ni el impacto de cada uno.

## 🧠 El Enfoque Curse Dev (Contra-pregunta)

* **Tú:** *"These two roles are often confused because they both work with data. Would it be useful if I explained the distinction in terms of what each role produces and who they serve?"*

## 💻 La Respuesta Técnica

La diferencia clave está en **qué construyen** y **para quién trabajan**:

| Dimensión | Data Engineer | Data Analyst |
|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| **Produce** | Pipelines, tablas limpias, infraestructura | Reportes, dashboards, insights |
| **Trabaja para** | Otros ingenieros, analistas, sistemas | Stakeholders de negocio |
| **Herramientas** | Python, SQL, Airflow, dbt, Spark | SQL, Tableau, Power BI, Excel |
| **Pregunta que responde** | ¿Cómo llegan los datos correctamente? | ¿Qué nos dicen los datos? |
| **Output** | Sistemas funcionando | Decisiones informadas |

**Analogía útil:**
- El Data Engineer construye la **cocina** (infraestructura, ingredientes preparados)
- El Data Analyst **cocina y sirve** (usa esos ingredientes para crear insights)

## 🇺🇸 El Script en Inglés (Para la entrevista)

> *"The key distinction is about what each role produces and who they serve. A Data Engineer builds and maintains the systems that make data reliable and accessible—pipelines, transformations, data models, and storage infrastructure. A Data Analyst consumes that prepared data to generate insights, reports, and recommendations for business stakeholders. Think of it this way: the engineer builds the kitchen and preps the ingredients; the analyst cooks the dish and serves it. In practice, the two roles are deeply interdependent—analysts depend on engineers for data quality, and engineers need analyst feedback to understand what data is actually needed."*

## 🔑 Vocabulario Clave

* **Pipeline:** Flujo automatizado de datos
* **Insight:** Hallazgo o conclusión accionable
* **Stakeholder:** Parte interesada en el negocio
* **Infrastructure:** Infraestructura de datos
* **Interdependent:** Interdependientes
